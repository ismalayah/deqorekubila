Overview of Berserk of Gluttony Season 2

The first season of “Berserk of Gluttony” consisted of 12 episodes and received positive feedback from fans. The show is set in a world filled with magic and monsters, following the story of Fate Graphite, a young man burdened with a unique curse. Fate possesses immense strength but must consume various materials to survive. Together with his childhood friend Chloe and a mysterious girl named Alicia, Fate embarks on a journey to find a cure for his affliction. The series combines elements of action and fantasy, creating a compelling and immersive storyline.

Storyline of Berserk of Gluttony

“Berserk of Gluttony” follows the journey of Fate Graphite, who is cursed with insatiable hunger. In order to survive, Fate must consume various materials, which grants him immense strength. As Fate explores a world filled with magic and monsters, he grapples with the consequences of his curse. Alongside Chloe and Alicia, Fate seeks a cure for his affliction.
