It was hard for her sister to keep her pregnancy a secret

Her sister Rihana is soon going to be a mom, but the news of her pregnancy was kept a secret for a long time, even from those close to her. 

Rihana confirmed the news of her pregnancy via her social media posts on 31st January 2022, where she shared she and her partnerA$AP were expecting their first child. 

It was a complete shock to the world. Rihana, during her Fenty Beauty and Fenty Skin Brands celebration event in February, shared how it was difficult to hide it from her close ones for a longer time and what it felt like when she found she was pregnant.

According to her, it was hard for her to hide but even harder for her to keep it from her posse because they were around her every time. 

Since they knew all her food habits, they were like, you don’t want something to drink? You are not drinking? And Rihana was eating all the things she was not used to eating and was suddenly asking for the cookies and donuts since she didn’t like sweets before. 

After all this, she finally had to let it out, all which she said was a complete shock for them as well as the world. 

Then she went on to share how she felt after learning about her pregnancy. She said when she first discovered about her pregnancy, she was in dilemma if it was real. 

After finding that it was real, she didn’t want to get too excited too soon since it was great news but at the same time wanted to see its way through. 

She also confessed to feeling glad that they were far along and now she could celebrate with everyone. 

Rihana dropped the legal case against their father, Ronald 

Rihana sued her father Ronald in 2019, alleging he misused her brand and relationship to launch his own business and violating advertising, competition, and privacy laws. 

According to the court documents, Ronald and his business partner Moses Perkins fraudulently created Fenty Entertainment claiming it was affiliated with Rihana in an attempt to solicit millions of dollars from unsuspecting third parties in exchange for the false promise. 

The case was due to come to trial on 22nd September 2021, but Rihana decided to dismiss the case. According to the sources, she reached an out-of-court settlement with her father and his business partner.
