Renato Mariotti Biography

Renato Mariotti is an American attorney, legal commentator, acting fill-in anchor for WGN-TV as well as a former federal prosecutor. On October 26, 2017, Renato announced his candidacy for Illinois Attorney General, but he lost in the Democratic primary election.

    Renato Mariotti Attorney General

    Renato ran as a Democrat to succeed Illinois Attorney General Lisa Madigan, who is retiring in the year 2018. In an interview with the Chicago Sun-Times Renato said his priority as attorney general would be economic justice:

    (People like my parents who are working hard as well as struggling to make ends meet, things haven’t gotten better for them. Since the ’80s or ’90s, they have continued to struggle, while people at the top who are taking advantage of them are getting more prosperous).

    Renato also pledged to focus on the problem of wage theft, estimating that over 50 million dollars in wages had been illegally withheld from workers in Illinois. Renato lost to State Senator Kwame Raoul in the March 20, 2018, primary election.
