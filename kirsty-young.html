Kirsty Young is a television and radio presenter from Scotland. She was the main presenter of BBC Radio 4’s “Desert Island Discs” from 2006 to 2018, and she previously hosted “Crimewatch” on BBC One from 2008 to 2015. Kirsty was appointed president of Unicef UK in February 2016. She was appointed a Director of Sussex Royal, The Foundation of the Duke and Duchess of Sussex, in October 2019 and served until the Foundation was dissolved in July 2020.
